F.A.Q.

Supporting Subheading

Do I need a driver's license to join?

Yes, all participants must hold a valid driving license. International visitors must bring either an International Driving Permit (IDP) or an officially approved translation, depending on your country.

Is it safe to drive on public roads?

Yes. Our tours follow strict safety guidelines, and a guide will lead the group at all times. A safety briefing is provided before departure.

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothing and closed-toe shoes are required. Avoid high heels, sandals, or loose items that may fall off during the ride.

What happens in case of rain?

Tours usually operate in light rain. We provide rain gear if needed. In case of severe weather, the tour may be rescheduled or canceled for safety reasons.

How many people can join per tour?

Each tour has a limited number of participants to ensure safety and a smooth experience. Please check availability when booking.

Can beginners join the tour?

Yes. No prior go-kart experience is required. Our guides will support you throughout the tour.

Can I take photos during the tour?

For safety reasons, taking photos while driving is not allowed. However, we will have stops where you can take photos, and guides may assist with pictures.
